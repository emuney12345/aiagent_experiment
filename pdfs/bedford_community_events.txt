TOWN OF Bedford - COMMUNITY EVENTS AND ACTIVITIES

Bedford COMMUNITY EVENTS CALENDAR

ANNUAL EVENTS OVERVIEW
Bedford hosts over 50 community events throughout the year
Events range from small neighborhood gatherings to large festivals
Most events are free and family-friendly
Special events calendar available at www.Bedford.gov/events

WEATHER POLICY
Outdoor events may be cancelled due to severe weather
Cancellation notifications posted on city website
Local radio and TV stations announce cancellations
Rain dates scheduled for major events when possible

PARKING AND TRANSPORTATION
Free parking available at all city events
Shuttle service provided for large events
Handicap accessible parking reserved near event sites
Public transportation available to most venues
