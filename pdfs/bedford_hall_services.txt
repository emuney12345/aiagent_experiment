SERVICES AVAILABLE

VITAL RECORDS
Birth certificates: $15 each
Death certificates: $15 each
Marriage licenses: $50 (valid 60 days)
Required documents: Valid photo ID, proof of eligibility
Processing time: Same day for most requests

BUSINESS SERVICES
Business license applications: $75 annual fee
Vendor permits: $25 per event
Liquor license applications: $200 processing fee
Sign permits: $50 per sign
Home occupation permits: $35 annual fee

PROPERTY SERVICES
Property tax payments accepted (cash, check, credit card)
Tax assessment appeals: October 1-31 annually
Property records searches: $5 per search
Homestead exemption applications: March 1-31 annually

UTILITY SERVICES
Water/sewer bill payments
Service connection applications
Meter reading disputes
Utility deposit arrangements: $100 for water, $50 for sewer
Budget billing plans available

BUILDING AND ZONING
Building permits: Residential $150, Commercial $300
Electrical permits: $75
Plumbing permits: $50
Zoning variance applications: $200
Historic preservation reviews: $100

RECREATIONAL SERVICES
Community center rental: $100 per day
Park pavilion rental: $75 per day
Athletic field rental: $50 per day
Swimming pool passes: $150 family, $75 individual
Senior center activities: $25 annual membership

SPECIAL SERVICES
Notary services: $2 per document
Passport applications: $25 processing fee (plus federal fees)
Voter registration: Free
Absentee ballot applications: Free
Public record requests: $0.25 per page

PAYMENT METHODS
Cash (exact change preferred)
Personal checks (with valid ID)
Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Discover)
Money orders
Online payments at www.Bedford.gov/payments

ACCESSIBILITY
All areas wheelchair accessible
ASL interpreters available with 48-hour notice
Large print documents available upon request
Hearing loop system in main meeting room

CITY COUNCIL MEETINGS
Regular meetings: First and third Tuesday of each month, 7:00 PM
Work sessions: Second Tuesday of each month, 6:00 PM
Location: City Council Chambers, Room 150
Public comment period: 30 minutes at start of each meeting
Agendas posted 48 hours in advance

PLANNING COMMISSION
Meetings: First Thursday of each month, 7:00 PM
Location: Conference Room A, Room 110
Public hearings for zoning and development issues
Applications due 30 days before meeting

ONLINE SERVICES
Available 24/7 at www.Bedford.gov:
- Pay utility bills and property taxes
- Apply for permits and licenses
- Report non-emergency issues
- Access city documents and forms
- Register for recreation programs
- View meeting agendas and minutes
